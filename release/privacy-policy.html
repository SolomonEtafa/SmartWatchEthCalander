Privacy Policy for Ethiopian Calendar

Effective date: June 15, 2026

Ethiopian Calendar, including the Ethiopian Chronograph watch face and Ethiopian Calendar Provider for Wear OS, respects your privacy.

Information collection

The apps do not collect, transmit, store, or share personal information. They do not require account registration and do not use advertising, analytics, tracking technologies, or remote servers.

Watch and complication data

The Ethiopian date is calculated locally on the watch using the date and time supplied by Android. Optional complications, such as health or heart-rate information, are selected and rendered by Wear OS. The apps do not independently access, collect, retain, or transmit health data.

Permissions

The apps do not request access to location, contacts, storage, camera, microphone, or other sensitive personal data.

Children's privacy

The apps do not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated if the apps' functionality or privacy practices change. The effective date above will be updated accordingly.
